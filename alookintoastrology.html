A look into Astrology.

Mar 21, 2024 Saloni Singh

Have you ever gazed at the night sky and wondered how the stars might influence your life? This is the essence of astrology, the ancient practice of studying the positions of celestial bodies and their supposed effect on human affairs. From love and career to personality traits and life paths, astrology offers a unique perspective on experiences.

Astrologers believe that the date, time, and location of your birth determine your natal chart, a celestial map unique to that moment. This chart consists of planets, the sun and moon, and the astrological houses they occupy. By interpreting the angles and interactions between these elements, astrologers aim to glean insights into your potential, strengths, weaknesses, and the overall themes that may color your life's journey.

The impact of astrology varies greatly. Some find it a source of comfort and guidance, using their horoscopes to navigate personal and professional decisions. For others, astrology sparks self-discovery and a deeper understanding of their inherent traits. However, it's important to remember that astrology is not a deterministic force dictating your fate.

Astrology is considered to be a powerful tool for self-reflection. By understanding the potential influences of the cosmos, individuals can gain a deeper appreciation for their strengths and navigate challenges with more awareness.

We humans need a belief system to survive. That belief can be any institution, god, science, anything. Astrology gives people, who have lost hope in life, something to hold on to. It offers a sense of direction and purpose. But any belief beyond a limit is never fruitful!

When taken too literally, astrology leads to a reliance on external forces rather than personal efforts. People dismiss opportunities or blame failures on their astrological profile. They think, “it’s in my chart, it will happen!”. They blindsight the words that your course of life changes according to the path and efforts you take. Astrology may give you some sense of direction but it’s eventually you who have to take the charge of your actions and sail your life. Additionally, focusing solely on negative aspects of a horoscope creates unnecessary worry or self-doubt in people who have a tendency to look at the negative aspects of things or who are discouraged in their life. It might snatch the hope and desire to proceed further to do good.

We should always remember every coin has two sides. If there's a good side to astrology then there's a bad side to it also exists. If there’s good astrologers who want to guide you then there’s malicious astrologers as well who prey on vulnerability. Be aware of what you listen to and who you listen to.

Astrology is a multifaceted practice with a long and complex history. Whether you find it a source of guidance or simply a fascinating cultural phenomenon, approaching it with a critical and open mind is key. Remember, astrology can be one lens through which we can view ourselves and the world around us, but it should not be the sole determinant of our choices or destinies.

There’s no scientific explanation for astrology. In fact, science denies it. My mind is still puzzled on how astrology works, how astrologers can be accurate with the personality traits and pasts of the people. I guess it’s gonna remain a mystery and point of speculation for the time being.
